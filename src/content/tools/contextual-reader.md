Contextual Reader is designed for deliberate, high-attention study sessions.

## Planned scope

- Layered annotation lanes for argument tracing.
- Progressive disclosure of references and footnotes.
- Session snapshots for uninterrupted return-to-context.

## Why it matters

The goal is not speed; it is comprehension with minimal cognitive noise.
