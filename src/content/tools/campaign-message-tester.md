Campaign teams can compare message variants and score readability in one workflow.

## What it does

- Scores reading complexity.
- Surfaces keyword overlap risks.
- Compares variants side-by-side.

## Why it matters

Teams avoid launching copy that is vague, dense, or misaligned with priority audiences.
