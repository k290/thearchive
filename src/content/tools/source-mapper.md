Source Mapper reveals relationships between ideas by tracing citation pathways across collections.

## Current features

- Citation graph visualization with clustering.
- Jump-to-source traversal for rapid verification.
- Filter modes for author, era, and domain.

## Roadmap

- Temporal replay of citation growth.
- Confidence scoring for inferred source links.
